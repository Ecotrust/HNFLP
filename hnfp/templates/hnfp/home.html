Coming together to create a shared vision for our lands.

The Hoonah Stewards application enables community members to participate in natural resource management decisions and improve communication with land managers. By providing localized information and tools for tracking personal activities, Hoonah Stewards can share information about what is important to the community.

What can you do as a steward?

Hoonah Stewards can track their hunting and gathering, get updates about road conditions, and receive notifications about important events. Stewards can choose to share private information with land managers if they desire. If you chose to share, all personal information is masked for privacy.

Take the survey that will help inform future land use decisions

Get notified when job opportunities related to land management arise

Keep track of your hunting/gathering locations, quanities, and dates

Notify/report alerts like road conditions so their repair can be prioritized
